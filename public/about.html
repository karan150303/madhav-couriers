Our Story

Madhav Couriers and Logistics is a Jalandhar-based courier company started in 2025 with a vision to revolutionize logistics services in Northern India. What began as a small local operation has grown into a trusted name for reliable shipping solutions across the country.

We provide safe, fast, and affordable logistics services with a focus on customer satisfaction, reliability, and transparency.

As a homegrown service based in Jalandhar, we understand the unique challenges of shipping in India. That's why we've built a network that covers all major cities and towns across the country, with special rates tailored for different regions and shipment types.

Our team is committed to delivering your parcels with the utmost care and efficiency. Whether you need document delivery, parcel shipping, or heavy logistics services, Madhav Couriers has the right solution for your needs with competitive pricing and reliable service.

At Madhav Couriers, we don't just deliver packages - we deliver peace of mind. Our customer-first approach means you can trust us to handle your shipments as if they were our own.
